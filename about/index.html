About

I am a research engineer at DSO National Laboratories, Singapore. I am currently visiting at the Natural Language Processing Group at CSAIL, MIT under Prof. Regina Barzilay.

I received a B.Sc (First Class Honours) from National University of Singapore in 2015 where I majored in Statistics & Applied Probability, with a specialization in business and finance statistics.

I am currently working on improving automated fact-checking. Previously, I have worked on projects that involved using correlated Gaussian Processes for heat modelling and online incremental clustering techniques for entity resolution.
